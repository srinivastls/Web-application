How to Register

  • The players have to create an account in this portal to register for any sport they are interested
  • Only the captain of a particular team has to create account to register his / her team in any sport
  • No team member other than captain is allowed to create account or register their teams
  • Your roll number and email address are compulsory for team registrations
  • Each player can choose a maximum of 3 games only among the available events
  • If a player is registered in two different teams in a single game, both the teams will be disqualified
